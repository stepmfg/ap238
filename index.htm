ABSTRACT: This document specifies an application interpreted model (AIM) based on the application reference model for machining defined by ISO 14649-10, ISO 14649-11, ISO 14649-12, ISO 14649-17, ISO 14649-111 and ISO 14649-121, augmented with ISO 10303 product geometry, geometric dimensioning and tolerancing and product data management information.

KEYWORDS: data exchange, computerized numerical controler (CNC), machine tool, machining, milling, turning, additive manufacturing, GD&T, digital twin, STEP-NC.
COMMENTS TO READER:

This is the fourth edition of AP238. This edition adds two units of functionality: additive manufacturing using powder-bed fusion, and drill and fill assembly with digital twins. It also adds common capabilities for broadcast of process state. The document has been harmonized with AP242e4 through shared use of the ISO 10303 resource parts collectively published as SMRLv12.

The new Annex K change log describes the changes in detail.
